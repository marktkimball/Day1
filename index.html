Introducing Fake Strava

Welcome to Fake Strava, the best knock off running site on the internet. At Fake Strava you will have the opportunity to track your running, races, and results against your friends. You can connect with your friends using Facebook and Twitter or search for them using the sidebar. Once you have your first friend on Fake Strava, you can compare race results and monthly mileage. Compete for bragging rights and personal records (PRs). Use the Races tab to find local or out of state races to run next.
